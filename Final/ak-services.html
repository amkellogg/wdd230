UX Design, or User Experience Design focuses on creating a space that is user friendly and easy to understand. As A UX Designer, I have studied and practiced to know how best to create a design that works well by itself, with other works, and is easy to navigate and understand. This could be with designing a website, an app, or even signs in an airport. UX design focuses on the interaction between the user and the product, assuring that the user can navigate the product with ease.
