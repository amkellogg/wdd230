Discover Napa

Napa has a wonderful community, you can gather with friends and family, or make new friends wherever you go!

Napa has beautiful feilds and vineyards that change color with the seasons.

Napa has wonderful open fields and parks to go explore.

There are many amazing drives and views around Napa, on a clear day in the mountains, you can see to San Fransisco!

Thanks to all the culture here in Napa, this town in blessed with world famous restaurants!

Have you ever seen the view from a hot air balloon? Now is your time!

Napa is surrounded by hills and mountians, thanks to this, you can find nature anywhere you go!

Just a short drive from Napa, you can visit the Veterans home and rome the grounds, giving thanks to those who have served our country.

Napa has long been known for farming, take a little bit to learn about the history!
