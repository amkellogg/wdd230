Napa Chamber of Commerce

napacommerce.com

The purpose of this webiste is to help the community members learn about their community. About their neighbors, local stores, monthly activities and fundrasers, and more.

The Target Audience

The target audience of this website is for the community members. They can visit this site to be able to find information about their communities and neighborhoods, upcoming events, and many other things.

Patricia Walsh

Patricia is a 30 year old new mom, she will want to visit the Chamber of Commerce website because she wants to find groups of families to be able to get together a play day with the local children in her neighborhood.

Steve Johnsons

Steve is a 23 year old back from college for the summer. He is looking for a mechanic job and wants to see if anyone is hiring or needs any help. He also is looking for any other locals looking to put togehter a basketball team in their free time.

Primary Color

This will be used as the primary color on the website. It is a darker blue which can help people feel calm and trust the site. It will be in the background of the website and also be used as the main headings.

Secondary Color

This secondary color will be used as other headers that are informational and go along with the first headings. It is a simple gray and can be easy to read.

Accent Color #1

This accent color will be used in the header and the footer of the page and can be found in the logo of the page.

Accent Color #2

This accent color will show up when any links are hovered over and can also be found on the logo.

Montserrat

This font will be used for the headings of the websire. It is a san serif which is simple and easy to read and see while also being professional.

Quicksand

The font Quicksand will be used where there are longer text segments such as the paragraphs. It will also be used for the links throughout the website. This font is also a san serif which will be easy to read and see and is a good font to use for large pieces of text, while it also matches the headings.
